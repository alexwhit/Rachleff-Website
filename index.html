Welcome to the Rachleff Scholars website! Rachleff scholars in an undergraduate research program in the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at the University of Pennsylvania. This program seeks to promote engineering research and encourage undergraduates to stay in the engineering profession.

For more information about the program and how to apply, check the About section. For information about the Rachleff Scholar Society and their activities, look at The Society and the About sections. For information on individual students and their projects, click on the Students page.
